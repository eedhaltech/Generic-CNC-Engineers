Our Story

BUILT IN COIMBATORE.
TRUSTED ACROSS INDIA.

Founded in 2004 in the industrial heartland of Coimbatore, Generic CNC Engineers began as a precision machining workshop serving the Tamil Nadu automotive and engineering belt. Over two decades, we have grown into a strong manufacturing force with dedicated production facilities and a presence across India.

Determined to offer a qualitative array of gearbox and automobile components, we have imbibed quality in each of our business operations. Our manufacturing processes are rigorously monitored through our total quality management system, ensuring every component meets the most demanding specifications.

Today, Generic CNC Engineers supplies leading OEMs and engineering companies across India. We don't chase volume — we chase precision. That philosophy has kept us in the supply chains of India's most demanding manufacturers for over two decades.
